Call for Papers

Boston University Metropolitan College invites submissions for the 20th Annual PMiP Conference Proceedings — 2026 Volume: Frontiers of Project Management. High-quality applied and scholarly contributions will be reviewed and presented at the conference. Accepted papers will be published in the official proceedings and selected papers may be assigned DOIs to enhance citation and scholarly visibility.

Who should submit? Both faculty and qualified graduate students are encouraged to submit full papers. Industry practitioners presenting at the conference may also submit papers based on their presentations. Priority for acceptance will be given to in-person attendees and presenters. A small number of papers may optionally be presented remotely.

Publication: Accepted papers will be published in the 2026 PMiP Annual Conference Proceedings. The Proceedings will serve as a permanent, citable record of the intellectual work shared at the conference and will be published in digital format.

Submission Topics

Submissions are invited in, but not limited to, the following areas:

Review Process

All submissions will undergo editorial review to ensure clarity, relevance, and professional quality. The Proceedings Editor may request revisions prior to final acceptance.

Authors will be notified of the review outcome following the full paper submission deadline. Revisions, if required, must be returned promptly to ensure inclusion in the final proceedings released on November 6, 2026.
