Call for Papers

Metropolitan College invites submissions for the 2026 PMiP Annual Conference Proceedings — Frontiers of Project Management. High-quality applied and scholarly contributions will be reviewed and presented at the conference.

Who should submit? Faculty, qualified graduate students, and industry practitioners presenting at the conference are all encouraged to submit. Priority for acceptance will be given to in-person attendees and presenters.

Submission topics include but are not limited to: Artificial Intelligence in Project Management, Ethics and Professional Responsibility, Construction Management & Technology, Digital Twins, Agile and Hybrid Methodologies, Project Risk Management, Leadership and Communication, Global and Cross-Cultural Environments, and Program & Portfolio Management.

Submission guidelines: Abstracts of 150–250 words are required for initial approval. Full papers must be 6–12 pages in APA format, submitted electronically in Microsoft Word. All submissions undergo editorial review. Accepted papers will be published in the 2026 PMiP Conference Proceedings and selected papers may be assigned DOIs.

Abstract Deadline: July 20, 2026  ·  Full Paper Deadline: August 4, 2026
