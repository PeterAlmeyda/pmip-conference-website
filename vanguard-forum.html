About the Vanguard Forum

Twenty years ago, a pioneering cohort of project managers walked out of Boston University and into industries they would quietly transform. Today, those vanguards return — not to reminisce, but to architect the future alongside the industry leaders they inspired.

This forum marks a historic convergence: for the first time since the program's founding, alumni from every graduating class — 2004 through 2024 — gather alongside the PMiP Industry Board in a living laboratory of strategy, reflection, and forward vision. The earliest graduating classes, who paved the way when project management in practice was still a frontier, return to BU to sit at the table with the board members now shaping the industry's next chapter.

Chaired by Mr. Wasim Al-Rashid, Chair of the PMiP Industry Board, the forum brings together two decades of field-tested expertise and macro-level industry oversight under one roof — a once-in-a-generation gathering that is both a celebration of where the program has been and a strategic blueprint for where it is going.

Forum Sessions

Six signature sessions structured around the program's 20-year arc, each designed to bridge alumni field experience with industry board strategy.

Session 01 · Opening Plenary

A Vanguard Synergy Session

Honoring the original graduates who paved the way 20 years ago and spotlighting their role as pioneers now collaborating with the board. "Vanguard" recognizes those who led the charge before the path was clear.

9:00 – 10:30 AM Plenary
Session 02 · Panel Dialogue

The Foundation & Future Forum

A balanced title positioning alumni as the program's bedrock and the Industry Board as architects of its future. A structured dialogue between where PMiP has been and where it is heading.

10:45 AM – 12:00 PM Panel
Session 03 · Signature Event

The Two-Decades Nexus

A modern, punchy session marking the literal connection point between 20 years of field history and elite industry oversight — where the program's legacy meets its strategic horizon.

1:00 – 2:15 PM Workshop Signature Event
Session 04 · Executive Roundtable

The PMiP Advisory Roundtable

A highly professional, executive-level session framing alumni and board members as a unified brain trust — candid, high-stakes strategic counsel in a closed roundtable format.

2:30 – 3:45 PM Roundtable
Session 05 · Working Group

The Industry–Alumni Co-Lab

An action-oriented working session for brainstorming new project frameworks, AI integration strategies, and emerging methodologies shaping the profession — alumni and board co-creating together.

4:00 – 5:00 PM Co-Lab AI Focus
Session 06 · Closing Summit

The Strategic Alignment Summit

Aligning 20 years of practical field experience with macro industry board oversight — culminating in a shared strategic declaration for PMiP's third decade.

5:00 – 6:00 PM Summit
