Speaker Profiles

Full bios for each featured speaker, including their session roles at this year's conference.

VK
Dr. Vijay Kanabar
Director of Project Management Programs; Associate Professor of Computer Science & Administrative Sciences
Boston University Metropolitan College
One of the earliest PMP-credentialed practitioners from the Project Management Institute, Dr. Kanabar has introduced several thousand students and practitioners to core project management concepts over three decades of teaching and applied research. A certified PMP with expertise spanning IT project management, electronic commerce, and information security, he has served as director of BU MET's long-running Project Management master's degree and graduate certificate programs. In 2017 he received the PMI Linn Stuckenbruck Teaching Excellence Award — widely regarded as the field's highest teaching honor — the first recipient ever selected from a global field of candidates. At PMiP 2026, Dr. Kanabar delivers the Opening Keynote and co-leads the Two-Decades Nexus signature workshop.
RM
Richard Maltzman
Master Lecturer, Administrative Sciences · Conference Co-Chair
Boston University Metropolitan College
Richard Maltzman is a Master Lecturer in Administrative Sciences at Boston University Metropolitan College and a certified Project Management Professional (PMP). He brings 38 years of industry experience at Nokia (previously Alcatel-Lucent and AT&T), where he rose from senior engineer to technical manager and served most recently as their learning and professional advancement leader, building and developing Nokia's Global Program Management Office. He holds an MS from Purdue University and a BSEE from the University of Massachusetts at Amherst. At PMiP 2026, he serves as Conference Co-Chair alongside Yolande Athaide, providing strategic direction and academic oversight for the 20th Anniversary conference.
GZ
Dr. Guanglan Zhang
Associate Professor & Chair, Department of Computer Science; Faculty Coordinator, Health Informatics Program
Boston University Metropolitan College
Dr. Guanglan Zhang leads the Health Informatics Research Lab at BU MET and serves as Chair of the Department of Computer Science — the department that houses BU MET's highly ranked MS in Computer Information Systems and MS in Applied Data Analytics programs. Her research expertise spans computational modeling of complex biological processes, biomedical data analysis, and the application of machine learning and analytics to healthcare systems. A co-inventor on two patents, she brings a rigorous data science lens to the intersection of project management and technology-driven domains. At PMiP 2026, Dr. Zhang moderates the Data-Driven Project Management panel, participates in the Foundation & Future Forum, and co-presents the Health Informatics Projects research session.
HK
Dr. Harold Kerzner
Senior Executive Director
International Institute for Learning (IIL)
A globally recognized authority on project, program, and portfolio management, Dr. Kerzner has spent over 50 years sharing guidance for making project management a strategic tool for competitive advantage, helping organizations around the world build foundations for excellence. He has authored or co-authored more than 60 textbooks and workbooks on the discipline, including seminal titles such as Project Management: A Systems Approach to Planning, Scheduling and Controlling and The Future of Project Management. His profound influence on the field is further recognized by the establishment of the Kerzner International Project Manager of the Year Award in his name. At PMiP 2026, Dr. Kerzner facilitates the Opening Plenary Vanguard Synergy Session and chairs the Executive PMiP Advisory Roundtable.
AN
Antonio Nieto-Rodriguez
Former Chairman, Project Management Institute; Author & Visiting Professor
PMI / Projects & Co / Strategy Implementation Institute
Author of the Harvard Business Review Project Management Handbook (2021) and the globally acclaimed HBR article "The Project Economy Has Arrived," Antonio Nieto-Rodriguez served as Chairman of the Project Management Institute in 2016 and co-founded the Brightline Initiative to help executives bridge the gap between strategic design and delivery. Recognized by Thinkers50 with its "Ideas into Practice" award and ranked among the Global Gurus Top 30, he teaches strategy execution to senior executives at Duke CE, IE Business School, Solvay, and Vlerick. His "Project Revolution" concept — arguing that projects are the core language of modern business — has reshaped how organizations at every level think about execution. At PMiP 2026, he moderates the Foundation & Future Forum panel dialogue and co-facilitates the Industry–Alumni Co-Lab working session.
RV
Ricardo Viana Vargas, PhD
Managing Partner; Former Chairman, PMI Board of Directors; Former Director, UNOPS Infrastructure & PM Group
Macrosolutions
The first Latin American elected Chairman of the Project Management Institute, Ricardo Viana Vargas has managed more than $20 billion in global initiatives across energy, infrastructure, technology, and humanitarian development over 25 years. As former Director of the United Nations Office for Project Services, he led more than 1,000 humanitarian and development projects worldwide. A recognized authority on AI-driven project management, he is co-author of the acclaimed HBR article "How AI Will Transform Project Management" and has written 16 books translated into seven languages. He received the PMI Fellow Award — the institute's highest honor. At PMiP 2026, he participates in the Foundation & Future Forum panel and delivers the closing talk on transformational leadership.
DH
Dr. David Hillson
International Risk Management Consultant; Director
The Risk Doctor Partnership
Known worldwide as "The Risk Doctor," Dr. David Hillson has worked across more than 50 countries and published 13 major books and over 100 journal papers on risk, earning global recognition as a leading thinker and expert practitioner in the discipline. He has developed numerous innovations in how risk is understood and managed that are now widely adopted as standard practice. A Fellow of the Project Management Institute, an Honorary Fellow of the UK Association for Project Management, and a Chartered Fellow of the Institute of Risk Management, he received the PMI Distinguished Contribution Award for his decades of work advancing risk management. His guiding motto — "Understand profoundly so you can explain simply" — defines a reputation for turning complex theory into actionable practice. At PMiP 2026, Dr. Hillson leads the hands-on Risk Management in Uncertain Environments workshop and participates in the Advisory Roundtable.
LB
Dr. Lynda Bourne
International Consultant, Author & Speaker on Stakeholder Engagement
Stakeholder Management Pty Ltd
An internationally recognized authority on stakeholder engagement and organizational PM maturity, Dr. Bourne is the creator of the Stakeholder Circle® visualization tool and author of Stakeholder Relationship Management: A Maturity Model for Organisational Implementation, which defines the SRMM® model now used by organizations across industries globally. A Fellow of the Australian Institute of Management and the Australian Computer Society, she was the 2003 winner of PMI Australia's Project Manager of the Year award and was included in PMI's inaugural list of the "25 Influential Women in Project Management." She has presented to audiences of project managers in IT, construction, defence, and mining across Europe, Asia, the United States, and Australia. At PMiP 2026, Dr. Bourne participates in the Executive PMiP Advisory Roundtable.
CF
Cornelius Fichtner, PMP, CSM
President & Founder; Host, The Project Management Podcast
OSP International LLC
An author, speaker, course creator, and host of the world-famous Project Management Podcast — the first podcast ever produced in the project management field — Cornelius Fichtner has over 20 years of PM experience across diverse industries and has helped more than 60,000 project managers prepare for and earn their PMP certification. A former PMI Chapter President and founding member of both PMI's New Media Council and Social Media Advisory Group, he has made PM education accessible and engaging to practitioners at every level. His flagship PM PrepCast remains one of the leading online PMP exam preparation tools in the world. At PMiP 2026, Cornelius co-leads the Two-Decades Nexus signature workshop alongside Dr. Vijay Kanabar.
